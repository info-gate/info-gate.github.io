Barolingo Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026 · Effective: April 19, 2026

wowpia ("we", "us", "our") values the privacy of Barolingo users. This policy explains what information we collect and how we protect it, in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1-1. On Signup (Required)

  • Social login identifier: kakao_id / google_sub / apple_sub
  • Email address
  • Nickname (provided by social account)
  • Profile image URL (if provided)

1-2. Automatically Collected During Use

  • Learning data: session start/complete, chunk reaction time (ms), accuracy, earned XP, streak
  • Device info: OS, app version, device model, language, timezone
  • Event analytics: screen views, login success, session events (via PostHog)
  • Crash logs: stack traces on error (via Sentry, when enabled)
  • Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID (for Free-tier ad serving via AdMob)

2. Purposes of Collection and Use

  • Member identification and session persistence
  • Storing learning progress and calculating level/ranking
  • Personalized content recommendations
  • Service quality improvement and error diagnosis
  • Ad serving (Free tier) and monetization
  • Legal compliance and dispute resolution

4. Data Retention

5. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Request processing restriction
  • Withdraw account (in-app Settings → Withdraw, or contact below)

6. Security Measures

  • Passwordless social OAuth (no passwords stored)
  • JWT-based auth, refresh tokens stored as SHA-256 hash
  • HTTPS in transit
  • Least-privilege access controls
